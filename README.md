In this project, I worked on analyzing employee performance data for INX Future Inc. to understand what actually drives performance inside a company. I explored the dataset to find patterns related to factors like department, experience, work environment, and salary, and tried to see how they impact performance ratings. After cleaning and analyzing the data, I built a few machine learning models such as Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and XGBoost to predict employee performance. Out of these, Random Forest gave the best results. Along the way, I found that things like work environment and experience play a much bigger role than just compensation. This project helped me understand how data can be used to make better HR and business decisions.
